## Research Focus

- Generalized action models for household robots.
- Context-aware manipulation actions such as cutting, pouring, transporting, and mixing.
- Scene graphs, domain knowledge, symbolic reasoning, and action cores for more autonomous robot behavior.
- Cognitive models and scalable algorithms for multi-step, context-dependent tasks.

## Selected Activities

- Research staff, Artificial Intelligence Group, University of Bremen.
- RoboCup@Home Technical and Organizing Committee Chair, 2024-2025.
- Research stay at the University of Miami, Department of Computer Science, 2025.
- Teaching and supervision in Suturo projects, KI-basierte Robotersteuerung, and Robot Programming with Lisp.
- Project involvement in EASE, Virtual Research Building, and RoboCup@Home.
